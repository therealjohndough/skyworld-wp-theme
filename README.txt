## Instructions for Integration

To maximize user engagement and keep traffic on our website, we strongly recommend embedding the COAs directly into the product pages. As our COAs are the most clicked link, this provides a seamless experience for our customers and keeps them on our site.

### Method 1: Simple Link (Good)

This is the most straightforward method. It opens the COA file in a new browser tab.

**Instructions:**

1.  Upload the PDF file to your website's asset or file directory.
2.  On the corresponding product page, add a link pointing to the file's URL.

**Example HTML:**
```html
<a href="URL_TO_YOUR_UPLOADED_PDF_FILE" target="_blank">View Certificate of Analysis</a>
